Use cases:

Understanding fish population and ocean health

Develop an open source FIWARE data space for ocean, fish population, map, water quality, and ecological forecasting data.

Tracking fishing boat movements

Simulate fishing trips from fishing docks to fish populations, running simulations to calculate the cost of seasonal fishing.

Monitoring fish species landed and their weight

Our goal is to use AI to demonstrate the value of a new aquaculture/fisheries economy based on a digital twin.

Tracking processing and delivery times

Integrate Open Street Map and Overpass APIs with fish populations as a digital twin to simulate the fishing industry, finding ways to optimize the supply chain with AI.

Providing net-zero waste solutions for seafood

Implement blockchain distributed technology for tracking fish creates traceable value for seafood.

Promoting innovation through community outreach, startup support, and IoT applications

Integrate with IoT devices and university research programs to build modern edge-to-cloud solutions to improve aquaculture and fishery observability and GitOps for resilience against storms.

Things you'll learn:

  1. How to deploy the Smart Aquaculture platform on OpenShift Local on your own computer.
  2. Set up Hashicorp Vault secrets for Smart Aquaculture with ExternalSecrets.
  3. Integrate with a FIWARE context broker for global standards for IoT device data with the Smart Aquaculture platform.
  4. Integrate with AI Telemetry, Prometheus Keycloak Proxy, and Keycloak Single Sign-On for seamless and secure metrics dashboards per user.
  5. Run Kubernetes Jobs to configure the database, configure Keycloak Realms and Clients, and configure all the secrets automatically.
  6. Set up role bindings between service accounts and secrets.
